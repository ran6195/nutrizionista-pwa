Chef's Nutritional Wisdom

1. The "Massage" Technique

Don't skip massaging the kale with a touch of olive oil and lemon. This breaks down the cellulose, making the nutrients more bioavailable and the texture silky.

2. Bioavailability Boost

The healthy fats in the avocado are essential for absorbing the fat-soluble vitamins (A, D, E, K) found in the kale and pomegranate. Nature provides the pairing; we just assemble it.

3. Crispness Secret

For the chickpeas, pat them completely dry before roasting. Any moisture will steam them instead of crisping them, losing that essential textural contrast.
